Our Story

Rooted in Temple Fairs, Evolving for the World

Fuzitenbai began as a humble stall near Kanda Myojin shrine, sourcing pickles and teas for festival-goers. Today we partner with over 200 artisans from Hokkaido to Okinawa, blending timeless hospitality with digital-first service.

Visit our flagship store in Nihonbashi to sample small-batch soy sauces, premium nori, and seasonal wagashi. Prefer to shop online? Our concierge team is available 24/7 via chat to recommend the perfect gift or pantry refill.
